description: Find deepening opportunities in a codebase — refactors that turn shallow modules into deep ones, informed by domain language and ADRs
---

Load the `improve-codebase-architecture` skill and follow its process:

1. **Explore** — Walk the codebase organically, noting architectural friction. Apply the deletion test to shallow modules.
2. **Present candidates** — Numbered list of deepening opportunities, each with files, problem, solution, and benefits.
3. **Grilling loop** — Once the user picks a candidate, walk the design tree with them, updating CONTEXT.md and ADRs inline as decisions crystallise.

Use CONTEXT.md vocabulary for domain terms and the skill's LANGUAGE.md vocabulary for architectural terms.
